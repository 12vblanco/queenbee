our Beekeeping courses:

Beekeeping Courses

If you would like to get started or try beekeeping or if you are looking for an original and exciting present our courses can teach you everything you need to know to make you a safe and successful beekeeper.
Our comprehensive courses are aimed at beginner medium level. We offer one-day courses and family / group discounts. All you need for the course can be provided by us, and we can even provide you with your own equipment to take home with you.
Visit our courses pager where you will find all the information, pictures, and videos about our courses as well as fees and dates.
